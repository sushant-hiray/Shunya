The Entrepreneurship Cell

The Entrepreneurship Cell is a non-profit organization run by the students of IIT Bombay that aims at manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of the young students. We at E-Cell hosts various workshops, speaker sessions, innovative games,competitions for aspiring entrepreneurs and support them by providing necessary resources such as seed funding, mentoring, consultancy and networking.

Our Initiatives

Eureka!: Asia's largest B-Plan Competition as recognized by Thomson Reuters.
E-Summit: The annual flagship event, a networking hub for corporates, professionals and budding entrepreneurs from all across the nation.
EnSpace: The Entrepreneurship magazine.
EnB-Club: This new initiative aims to create an ecosystem for the budding entrepreneurs in the institute.
